Football clubs

Real madrid

Real Madrid, founded in 1902, is one of the most successful football clubs in history, known for its record 14 UEFA Champions League titles. Based in Madrid, Spain, the club plays at the iconic Santiago Bernabéu Stadium and has a global fanbase.

Milan

AC Milan is one of Italy's most successful football clubs, known for its rich history and iconic red and black colors. The club has won 7 UEFA Champions League titles and plays its home matches at the renowned San Siro Stadium in Milan, Italy.

Barcelona

FC Barcelona, founded in 1899, is one of the most successful and iconic football clubs in the world. Known for its attacking style and strong Catalan identity, Barça has won numerous domestic and international titles.

Liverpool

Liverpool FC, founded in 1892, is one of England's most successful football clubs with a rich history and passionate fanbase. Known for their iconic anthem "You'll Never Walk Alone," Liverpool has won numerous domestic and international trophies, including multiple European Cups.

Manchester City

Manchester City FC, founded in 1880, is a top English football club known for its recent dominance in both domestic and European competitions. Under the management of Pep Guardiola, the club has won multiple Premier League titles and established itself as a force in modern football.

Manchester United

Manchester United Football Club, founded in 1878 as Newton Heath LYR F.C. and renamed in 1902, is one of the most successful and popular football clubs in England. With a record 20 English top-flight league titles and 3 UEFA Champions League trophies, the club plays at Old Trafford and has a global fanbase.
